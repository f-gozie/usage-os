Brand system · the chosen mark
Branding around “Contexts”

The logo is the dial itself — a day inked in its categories (Work blue, Browsing red, Messaging gold) over a faint idle track, with a clean gap at midnight up top. It’s the only mark that is the product. Below: the system, then it dropped onto every surface the app ships through. Switch themes (top-right) to see it hold on paper / warm / black.

01 · The mark
One mark, four states
Primary
Color, on light. The default.
On ink
Color holds on dark too.
Mono
One ink — menubar, favicon, etch.
+ Now-hand
Accent: the triangle at midnight.
  • Idle track — the faint full ring; the day not yet spent.
  • Work — the longest block, just past midnight.
  • Browsing — the afternoon arc.
  • Messaging — the evening arc, back toward midnight.
  • Now-hand — optional triangle at the midnight gap.

Clear space

Keep clear space ≥ the arc-stroke weight on every side. Don’t crowd it.

Below ~24px the three colors muddy — switch to the mono template. The gaps still read as “a dial.”

The honest caveat with a multi-color mark: it can’t carry color into a 16px menubar slot. Solved — the mono template keeps the three gaps, so it still reads as the segmented dial in a single ink.
