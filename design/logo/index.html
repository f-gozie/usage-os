A mark for usageos

No real logo exists yet — the landing’s glowing cyan ◈ is leftover cyberpunk, off-brand against our Bauhaus system. This is a wide first pass: many directions, many variants, so we can react and narrow. Every mark is drawn to live in mono (menubar / favicon), in the primary palette, and on ink — across all three themes (try the switcher).

What it has to carry

  • The dial is the soul — a 24h chronometer, midnight at top, a now-hand. The mark should grow from it.
  • Calm, private, rear-view — not a productivity-app clock screaming at you.
  • Reads at 16px in flat black for the menubar, and as a bold app-icon tile.

The Bauhaus kit it’s built from

  • Circle · triangle · square — which already map to dial · now-hand · window.
  • Primary inks — blue #1B45BE, red #E0241B, gold #EAB308, on bone / ink.
  • Anton + Jost, hard edges, flat fills. No glow, no gradient, no rounded accent.

on the nose?

My instinct: 01 Midnight, 03 Slice, and 05 Notch are the safest bets — they survive 16px, work in one ink, and are unmistakably “the dial.” 04 U-gauge is the most ownable if we want a true monogram. 02 Contexts is the best “hero” mark for the website where color is free.
