Privacy

Nothing leaves this machine.

It reads the frontmost window's title and the current browser tab, and writes them to one file on your Mac. There's no network in the data path — and it's open source, so you can read the code and confirm it. Here's exactly what it leaves alone:

SystemIncognito & private windowsnever recorded🔒 Always on
AutoPassword managers & bankingexcluded out of the boxExcluded
AppAnything you excludedropped entirely, never storedExcluded
TitleAnything you mark privatethe time still counts, the title doesn'tPrivate

Your history is one SQLite file — reveal it in Finder, export it to CSV, set it to auto-delete after 30/90/365 days, or wipe it. It can't see your screen.
