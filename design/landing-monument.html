Privacy

Nothing leaves this machine.

It reads your window titles and the current browser tab, and writes them to a database on your Mac. There’s no network in the data path at all — and it’s open source, so you can confirm that yourself. It never uses Screen Recording, skips private windows, and auto-excludes password managers and banking. No cloud. No account. No telemetry.
