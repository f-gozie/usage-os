Features
What UsageOS does
The whole day, one dial
Your last 24 hours as a chronometer. You can see the shape of your day — the focus, the drift, the gaps.
A recap, not a spreadsheet
Each evening, a few honest sentences about where your time actually went — written for you, on your machine.
Smart, but local
Optional on-device AI sorts your time and writes the recap. The model runs on your Mac — never on a server.
Private by design
No cloud, no account, no telemetry. Nothing about your day ever leaves this Mac.
